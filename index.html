Let's Play

Intro:

Rock, Paper, Scissors is a hand game usually played between two people, in which each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with an outstretched hand. It is a simple game and is often played as a two-player discrete game, but may also be played online or played with multiple players simultaneously. It is a popular children's game in many countries with many different names.

Rules:

  1. Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock.
  2. In each round, players simultaneously create shapes with their outstretched hands, and the winning shape is typically the one that completely covers the other.
  3. For example,
    • a hand forming a rock beats a hand forming a paper,
    • a hand forming a paper beats a hand forming a scissors,
    • a hand forming a scissors beats a hand forming a rock.
